1 # IMU Orientation Convention
2 
3 dRonin defines vehicle axes as X forward, Y right, Z down. Bottom sensor orientation is +180 degrees about vehicle X-axis, and is applied first. Sensor rotations are about the vehicle Z-axis, e.g. top 90 degrees is rotated 90 degrees clockwise looking top-down. Note: top 0 degrees is defined with the pin 1 marker closest to the front left corner of the board.
4 
5 ## Invensense IMUs
6 All known Invensense IMUs share the same gyro/accelerometer convention (with pin 1 at front-left corner): X right, Y forward, Z up. The AKxxxx magnetometer inside MPU9xx0 matches dRonin vehicle convention (X forward, Y right, Z down).
